## **\[THE COLLAPSE OF THE IRON TRADE\]**

Within six months of the Queen's ascension, the global iron market ceased to exist.

* **The "Recall":** Miners reported that veins of ore literally retreated deeper into the earth when approached.  
* **The "Rot":** Surface-forged steel began to develop "resonance-leprosy"—a vibration-based fatigue that causes metal to shatter like glass when used for violence.

## **\[THE AUTOMATON DIASPORA\]**

The "Them" units did not return to the Deep. They redistributed.

* **The Wardens:** Giant, bipedal units now stand stationary at every major mountain pass. They do not attack; they simply *hum*. This frequency prevents the transport of heavy siege weaponry.  
* **The Tenders:** Smaller units (like Unit-88) have been seen "healing" the landscape, using Mithril-seeding to reinforce collapsing cliffs and purifying water sources tainted by industrial runoff.

## **\[THE ARCHITECTURAL REVOLUTION\]**

Humanity has been forced to adapt to "Non-Metallic Engineering."

* A surge in high-density masonry and glass-blowing has replaced traditional blacksmithing.  
* Cities are becoming quieter. Without the clang of the forge, the "Hum" of the Sea-Keep can be heard even hundreds of miles away on a still night.

## **\[THE CULTURAL SHIFT\]**

A new religion has formed around the "Mithril Queen." Pilgrims travel to the three-mile "Dead Zone" just to leave offerings of unrefined stone, hoping to hear a single note of the Song.

**The world is no longer ruled by those who sharpen the blade, but by those who can listen to the earth.**
