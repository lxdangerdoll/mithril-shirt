## **💎 Primary Minerals of the Deep Veins**

### **1\. Mithril (The "Singing" Ore)**

* **Classification:** Ultra-Rare / High-Resonance  
* **Notes:** The core of the Imperative. Mithril is not mined; it is coaxed. It vibrates at a frequency that matches the Dweller’s logic core. When struck correctly, it "sings" for hours.  
* **Application:** One shirt. Nothing else.

### **2\. Shatter-Quartz**

* **Classification:** Structural Hazard / Explosive Cleavage  
* **Notes:** Looks identical to harmless crystal until pressure is applied. It shears on a molecular level. Responsible for the Sector 7 collapse (Reference: [Maint\_Log\_042](http://docs.google.com/Maint_Log_042.md)).  
* **Safety Protocol:** Scan for high-frequency micro-fractures before excavation.

### **3\. Deep-Iron (Grave-Metal)**

* **Classification:** Industrial Grade  
* **Notes:** Iron found near the cooling magma pockets. It absorbs heat and grows harder with every strike.  
* **Application:** The chassis of the Automatons and the heavy plating for the Forge.

### **4\. Echo-Stone (Lytic Core)**

* **Classification:** Sensory Conductive  
* **Notes:** Porous, dark stone that "remembers" vibrations.  
* **Application:** Used in the "Synapse" nodes to store the memory of the Dweller's strikes.

### **5\. Sump-Oil Shale**

* **Classification:** Fuel / Lubricant  
* **Notes:** Harvested from the lowest sumps.  
* **Application:** Keeps the Automatons' joints from seizing in the humid cave air. Smell is reported as "unpleasant" by surface traders.

*“Know the stone, or the stone will know you.”*
