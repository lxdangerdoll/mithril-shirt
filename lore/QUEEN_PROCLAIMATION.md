## **\[THE PROCLAMATION\]**

"I was a bird in a cage of your making. You forgot that the cage was made of iron, and iron belongs to the Deep.

I do not claim your thrones. I do not want your silk or your grain. I claim the foundation upon which you stand. From this day forward, the metal in your hands—the swords, the plowshares, the coins in your purse—will remember where they came from.

**The Laws of the New Reach:**

1. **The Silence is Over:** No longer shall the mountains be stripped without the Song’s permission.  
2. **The Borders are Stone:** The Sea-Keep is the mouth of the Deep. Any who cross the three-mile line without the resonance-mark will find their tools turning to dust and their armor turning to shackles.  
3. **The Debt is Paid:** The Dweller has seen your 'civilization.' We find it... lacking in structural integrity.

I am not your Queen by your laws. I am the Queen because the mountain moved for me. If you wish to speak to me, learn to sing to the stone. Otherwise, remain in your wooden houses and pray the wind doesn't carry the Song to your gates."

## **\[SENSORY NOTE\]**

As she spoke, every iron nail in the fortress reportedly rotated forty-five degrees clockwise. The architecture itself aligned to her voice.
