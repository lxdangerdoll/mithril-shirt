## **\[THE NEW WEAPONS\]**

Since the "Iron Rot" has rendered steel useless for aggression, the Coalition has turned to the "Old Ways" with a modern twist.

* **The Glass-Walkers:** A specialized unit equipped with high-impact ceramic armor and obsidian-edged blades. They are fragile, but they do not vibrate when the Queen sings.  
* **Chemical Alchemy:** Experiments are underway to create "liquid stone"—a fast-hardening resin that can be fired from wooden catapults to encase the Warden units.

## **\[THE BONE-SCRIPT\]**

Resistance cells have stopped using digital or metallic communication. They are using "Vocal-Proofing"—a series of whistles and percussion-based codes that mimic the natural sounds of the forest to avoid detection by the Mithril-Sensing grid.

## **\[MISSION\_OBJECTIVE: 'SILENCE\_THE\_SINGER'\]**

Intelligence suggests a plan to infiltrate the Sea-Keep using "The Blind Fold"—a dampening field made of layers of lead-lined silk and sound-absorbent moss.

* *Risk Assessment:* The Queen doesn't just hear sound; she feels structural integrity. A human heart is a rhythmic structure. Can she "feel" a heartbeat?
